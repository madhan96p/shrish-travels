Transparent & Competitive Cab Tariffs

At ShRish Travels, we believe in clear and upfront pricing. We offer competitive rates for drop trips, round trips, and local packages across Tamil Nadu, Pondicherry, Karnataka, Kerala, and Andhra Pradesh. Our commitment is to provide value by charging only for what you use—no return fare on one-way drops, and no hidden fees!

Outstation Trips: Drop & Round Trip Rates

Vehicle Type Drop Trip (₹/km) Round Trip (₹/km)
Sedan (e.g., Dzire, Etios) ₹15 ₹12
MUV (e.g., Ertiga, Marazzo) ₹18 ₹16
SUV (e.g., Innova) ₹19 ₹16

Important Notes for Outstation Trips:

  • Rates are inclusive of GST.
  • Drop Trip Driver Batta: ₹400 (₹600 if trip is above 300km).
  • Round Trip Driver Batta: ₹600 per day.
  • Waiting Charges: ₹150 per hour (first hour may be complimentary based on booking).
  • Minimum Billing: 100 km for Drop Trips, 250 km per day for Round Trips.
  • Hill Station Surcharge: ₹300 (Sedan), ₹500 (MUV/SUV).
  • Pets: A nominal cleaning charge of ₹400–₹500 may apply if required.
  • Excess Luggage: Charges may apply for oversized or excess luggage (₹300).
  • Pick-up/Drop Points: Includes 2 standard points. Additional points may incur extra charges.
  • Toll fees, parking charges, and inter-state permits are applicable at actuals and are not included in the above rates.
